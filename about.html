About us

OUR INSTITUTE

Buhle Catering College is a vocational training institute aimed at providing students with the best catering and culinary skills to meet local and international standards. We provide all students with a school T-shirt, branded with the institution logo, and stationary at no additional costs. Students are required to attend classes in plain black pants or skirts [More than 45cm Long], and black closed flat shoes.

We also provide students with practical training where all students are required to wear their full chef gear when attending them. We are fully qualified to train people even if they do not have any qualifications in catering or computer.
