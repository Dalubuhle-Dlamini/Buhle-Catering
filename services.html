Our Services

WE OFFER A WIDE VARIETY OF SERVICES


At Buhle Catering College, we offer a wide variety of services although our main business is providing quality catering courses and other hospitality courses in general. We do offer catering services to reputable hotels in Eswatini . Moreover, we do offer training to our students and due to the hands on experience, they then offer high quality services to hotels that they are attached to during the internship period. Buhle Catering College is a reputable organization and therefore our staff members are also hired to offer catering services to reputable hotels in the country and abroad. Our highly qualified staff members also provide other services such as baking cakes for a wide variety of occasions, catering services for any kind of occasion that hosts up to 1000 guests. Other than these services, we also consider offering internships to students from our institution and from other institutions alike.
