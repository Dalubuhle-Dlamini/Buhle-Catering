Our Story

Buhle catering college is a modern learning institution located in Mbabane, Manzini and Nhlangano. Here at Buhle Catering College we specialize in teaching hospitality courses such as cookery courses, pastry, housekeeping, food and beverage management and basic computer courses.

The Education division of the group has evolved from offering multiple programs including Information and Technology (ICT) courses, being a proficient user of Microsoft application including word.
